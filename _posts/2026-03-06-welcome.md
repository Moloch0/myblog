This is the first sample post.

For new notes, add Markdown files to `_posts/`, for example:

`2026-03-07-my-note.md`

with front matter:

```yaml
---
title: "Your Title"
date: 2026-03-07 21:00:00 +0800
categories: [study]
tags: [llm, notes]
---
```

The site will automatically:

- sort posts by date
- group content by categories
- group content by tags
