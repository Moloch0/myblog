## 3. Repo Structure Conventions

- Published content source of truth: `_posts/`
- Engineering docs: `docs/`
- Theme/frontend extensions: `_includes/`, `assets/`
- Ops scripts: `tools/`

## 4. Dev and Release Entry Points

- Local run: `bash tools/run.sh`
- Local test: `bash tools/test.sh`
- Deploy pipeline: `.github/workflows/pages-deploy.yml`
- Pre-release checks: `docs/RELEASE-CHECKLIST.md`
